About

Welcome to our Venue Booking System, where you can easily book venues for your events. Our system offers a wide range of venues to choose from, ensuring that you find the perfect space for your event needs.

Booking Rules

  • Bookings are accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Each venue can be booked for a maximum of 8 hours per day.
  • Bookings can be made up to 30 days in advance.
  • Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to the booking date.
